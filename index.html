Mel's Turkish Kitchen

Cooking Classes in Fethiye, Türkiye

Learn to cook delicious, authentic Turkish meals!

  • Mel's cooking classes offer instruction for beginners and experienced chefs alike. The 4-hour class is offered as a day program starting at 2pm in the afternoon, every Thursday. .
  • Mel is an experienced chef, cooking delicious dishes since 1993. Since then, she’s proudly hosted students from over all over the world. We encourage you to try Mel’s culinary experience and be pleasantly surprised at how fulfilling and informative our classes can be in learning how to make delicious and authentic Turkish dishes.
  • The classes are held in a large kitchen, hosting up to 8 people. Bookings can be made through the contact details below.

Class Structure

Class Introduction

During this time students get acquainted with the instructor and fellow students.

Main Lesson - Cooking

During the main lesson time you will learn to cook multiple recipes, following along as Mel demonstrates. During the class you will learn the traditions and stories behind each dish. The recipes change every week, using fresh produce and quality ingredients - (if you have any allergies, please inform us before the lesson).

Closing Discussion and Meal

Enjoy your freshly cooked food as you chat class about what you've learned, with time for any further questions for Mel.
